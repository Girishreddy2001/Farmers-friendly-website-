Chilli cultivation

  • Range between 20⁰-25⁰C is ideal temperature range for chilli growth. At 37⁰C or higher the crop development is affected.
  • Chillies need moisture for growth.
  • pH of soil should be between 6.5 and 7.5 (neutral soil). It cannot tolrate acidic nor alkaline soil.
  • Sowing months are May to June for Kharif crop, September to October for Rabi crops.

Carrot cultivation

  • Garden-grown carrots are full of flavor and texture! They are a popular, long-lasting root vegetable that can be grown in many climates

  • Carrots are easy to grow as long as they are planted in loose, sandy soil during the cooler periods of the growing season—spring and fall (carrots can tolerate frost).

  • Depending on the variety and local growing conditions, carrots may take anywhere from 2 to 4 months to mature.

  • They are attacked by pests such as Black (Itersonilia) canker,Carrot rust flies,Flea Beetles,Root-knot nematodes,Root-knot nematodes,Wireworms

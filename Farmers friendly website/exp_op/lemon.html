Lemon cultivation

  • Lemons can be grown in all types of soils. Light soils having good drainage are suitable for its cultivation.
  • PH range of soil should be 5.5-7.5. They can also grow in slightly alkaline and acidic soils.
  • light loam and well drained are the most suitable for lemon cultivation. Lemons are the productive crops, where they start producing the fruits from 4th year and will sustain a productive life of 15-20 years.
